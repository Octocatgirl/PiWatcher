PiWatch Security was created by two teen engineers with Python. Our goal is to utilize machine learning to create safer security for your home or business. Through machine learning we train the computer to detect authorized people and to alert when there is danger near your property. Currently we are utilising the raspberry pi camera for security detection. We hope to later expand to comercial use security camera’s that can utilize our software to make things more safe.

Detection

When there is someone nearby PiWatch detects the intruder with the pi motion sensor. It fires up is recognition program to check if the intruder is safe or not.

Recognition

With our machine learning software, the computer can recognise if the intruder is someone you deem safe or is unknown. It gives a percentage on how likely this person is a registered safe person. The more that our software seen known people the better it becomes at recognising who is safe and who is not.

Notification

Notifications are sent if PiWatch identifies an unknown intruder. They receive a email notifying them that there is danger, letting you know you need to check your camera to make sure your home or proper is safe!
